If we don’t do this now, our children will

The family story of the fallen Hero of Ukraine Bohdan Karvatko

At the invasive war of russian federation against Ukraine, a lot of soldiers desperately sacrificed their lives by fighting bravely for the freedom of their motherland and its people. Russian aggression came into the family of a military woman Katherina and took away her husband’s life and the father of two children, Ukrainian military Bohdan Kravatko, the most precious person in her life.

  • The story of how the couple met each other

    Katherina met her future husband at the thematic online community, when he was only a cadet. The woman is recalling with a smile on her face that for the first date Bohdan came 40 minutes later and she says, “Maybe, it was the happiness I was waiting for”.

  • A dream to become a real officer

    The wife of the fallen military is telling that her husband had dreamt to become an officer since his childhood. This was due to his uncle who was a military man and who became Bohdan’s role model. His dream came true and everyone who knew Bohdan said he was a real officer and a man of his word. The military man sincerely wished to impact the Ukrainian army in its modernization and development, get rid of Soviet principles, get new perspectives for both future defense structure and himself


The story of Bohdan Karvatko’s heroism and death

The military man wanted to go to ATO to protect his land in 2014, but he appeared at the front line only in 2022, when the russian full scale invasion at Ukraine started. At first, he was protecting Kiev region and then he moved to the East as a part of a tactical battalion group. Bohdan never stopped thinking about a duty to protect his country, for he understood “if we don’t do this, then in 20 or 10 years our children will do this”. Katherina remembers the last talk with her husband. He said only one thing, “It’s hell here”. In that hell, Bohdan and some of his comrades, fighting for the freedom of their motherland and the peaceful future of their children, did not retreat. They did not want to let the enemy do a break through and they died while doing their duty.

For a mother and a father – how a widow was learning how to live

The hardest times came to the Katherina’s family after her husband’s death. At first, the woman and her children did not know where their home was, because they were forced to move from place to place all the time. Their life can hardly be called stable. Katherina could hardly handle her grief and not be pulled apart. But despite the painful loss, she knew there can be no break in her life and she had to work and, even more important, raise her children. “I have to think about their future and safety, and our little family”, - the woman says.


The most important thing in your life is your family
At the same time Katherina understood quite well that she also needed to take care of herself, because her mental state was very important for the harmony in her family. Soon Katherina and her children succeeded in dealing with the difficulties and handled their life, but the loss of the closest person hurt. The woman says, “Only when you lose your close person, you understand how happy you used to be and that actually nothing else matters. The most important thing in your life is your family and close people by you”. Katherina also tells that her husband had got medals, awards, and a title of the Hero of Ukraine and Gold Star from the president. The woman is willing to save all Bohdan’s awards to let his future great-grandchildren know that their father, grandfather was a real hero of his country, a hero who fought for their peaceful future in Ukraine that will be free.

A dream of the fallen military man we can fulfill

When Bohdan was still alive, he dreamt of his children getting educated and being able to develop. Katherina is pursuing this dream too. Together we can make this dream come true by donating to Katherine’s family giving an honor for Bohdan Karvatko, a real officer and Hero of Ukraine.
